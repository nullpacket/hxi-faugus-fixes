Why d3d8to9 (and not DXVK's d3d8): DXVK's d3d8 is included in recent Proton
releases (enabled via PROTON_DXVK_D3D8=1) but causes a privileged-instruction
crash within ~30 minutes for FFXI/Ashita — likely a timing incompatibility
with Ashita's d3d_present hooks. d3d8to9 (which translates D3D8 calls to
D3D9 so DXVK's d3d9 backend can handle them) is stable for FFXI.
